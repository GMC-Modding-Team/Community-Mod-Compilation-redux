## Mod Contents

### 1. Weapons and Equipment

- Created British military firearms (L85A2 rifle, L9A1 pistol, L7A2 GPMG)
- Added British military armor (DPM and MTP uniforms, Osprey body armor, Mk7 helmet)

### 2. NPCs with Dialogue

- British soldiers with appropriate dialogue
- British officers with command dialogue
- SAS operators with tactical dialogue
- RAF pilots with aviation-themed dialogue

### 3. Locations

- British military outposts
- Larger military compounds with NPCs
- RAF airfields with appropriate structures

### 4. Professions

- British Army Infantry
- Royal Marine Commando
- SAS Operator
- RAF Pilot

### 5. Missions

- Created a mission chain for the British officer NPC:
  - Find a military radio
  - Retrieve medical supplies
  - Eliminate a zombie officer

### 6. Food and Recipes

- Added British tea (with and without milk)
- Created British military ration packs
- Added recipes for crafting British military gear
